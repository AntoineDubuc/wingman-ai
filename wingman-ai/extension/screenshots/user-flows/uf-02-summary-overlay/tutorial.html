UF-02: View and Interact with Summary Overlay

Wingman AI -- Summary overlay lifecycle and interaction walkthrough

1
Loading State Appears

When a session ends, the summary overlay appears in the top-right corner showing a "Generating Summary..." loading state with an animated indicator and the message "Analyzing your conversation..."

Verify: A dark overlay card appears in the top-right with "Generating Summary..." title, a pulsing dot indicator, and "Analyzing your conversation..." subtitle.
2
Summary Card Appears

Once Gemini returns the summary, the overlay transitions to display the full "Call Summary" card. It shows a SUMMARY section with bullet points, an ACTION ITEMS section with labeled items (YOU/THEM badges), a collapsed KEY MOMENTS section, and a Copy button.

Verify: The card header reads "Call Summary" with a green dot and close (X) button. The SUMMARY section lists key discussion points. ACTION ITEMS show orange "YOU" and teal "THEM" badges. KEY MOMENTS (3) is collapsed with a triangle indicator.
3
View Action Items

The ACTION ITEMS section is visible by default within the summary card. Each item is labeled with a badge indicating responsibility -- "YOU" (orange) for your follow-ups and "THEM" (teal) for the prospect's commitments.

Verify: Action items are displayed with clear responsibility labels. Example items: "Send detailed pricing quote by Friday" (YOU), "Share SOC2 compliance report" (YOU), "Loop in CTO for technical deep-dive" (THEM).
4
Expand Key Moments

Click the "KEY MOMENTS (3)" header to expand the section. Notable quotes and buying signals from the conversation are revealed, each with a colored left border indicating significance.

Verify: The KEY MOMENTS section is expanded (downward triangle). Three quoted moments are shown: "We have budget approved for Q2" (green border), "We are also evaluating Competitor X" (red border), "Let's schedule the CTO call for next Tuesday" (yellow border).
5
Click Copy Button

Click the "Copy" button at the bottom of the summary card. The button text changes to "Copied!" to confirm the summary has been copied to the clipboard.

Verify: The button label changes from "Copy" to "Copied!" providing immediate visual feedback that the summary text was copied to the clipboard.
6
Copy Button Reverts

After a brief delay, the "Copied!" button reverts back to "Copy", ready for another click. This transient feedback pattern avoids permanent UI changes.

Verify: The button text has reverted from "Copied!" back to "Copy". The button is fully interactive and can be clicked again.
7
Drive Save Indicator

If Google Drive integration is connected, a "Saved to Drive" status indicator appears at the bottom-right of the summary card, confirming the transcript has been auto-saved.

Verify: A green "Saved to Drive" text label appears in the bottom-right corner of the summary card, next to the Copy button. This confirms the transcript was auto-saved to Google Drive.
8
Close Overlay

Click the close (X) button in the top-right corner of the summary card. The overlay is dismissed and the underlying page is fully visible again.

Verify: The summary overlay is completely removed. The Wingman Settings page is fully visible with no overlay elements remaining.
