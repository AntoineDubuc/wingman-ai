Conclave Presets

Save persona combinations as presets for instant switching and set a leader to guide summaries

1
Open the Conclave Tab

Go to Settings and click the Conclave tab. Here you can configure how multiple personas work together and save persona combinations as reusable presets.

What's a Conclave? A conclave is a saved group of personas that work together. Think of it as a "team preset" you can activate with one click.
2
Set the Conclave Leader

The Conclave Leader determines whose perspective shapes the post-call summary. Choose the persona whose expertise is most relevant to your call goals.

Example: For a sales call, set Sales Closer as leader. The summary will focus on deal progression, objections handled, and next steps rather than technical details.
3
Create a New Preset

Click "+ New Preset" to create a new conclave. Give it a name, select which personas to include, and save. The preset shows colored dots for each included persona.

Preset ideas: "Sales Team" (Sales Closer + Technical Expert), "Enterprise Deal" (add Legal & Compliance), "Discovery Only" (just Discovery Coach).
4
Activate from Popup

Open the popup and click "Conclave presets" dropdown. Select a preset to instantly activate all its personas. No need to add them one by one.

5
Manage Your Presets

Back in the Conclave tab, you can edit or delete presets. Each preset shows its persona count and included personas. Click "Activate" to switch without opening the popup.
