Features

Everything You Need During a Call

Live transcription, contextual AI suggestions, and emotion awareness — all running silently alongside your conversation.

🎤

Live Transcription

Real-time speech-to-text via Deepgram Nova-3. See what everyone says as they say it, with configurable pause sensitivity.

🤖

AI Suggestions

Contextual suggestions powered by Gemini, OpenRouter, or Groq. Your wingman surfaces the right thing to say, when it matters.

💡

Emotion Detection

Hume AI reads vocal tone in real-time. Know when the other person is frustrated, engaged, or thinking — before they tell you.

👤

Multi-Persona System

Run up to 4 AI personas at once. Each expert listens independently and only speaks up when their specialty is relevant.

📚

Knowledge Base

Upload PDFs, Markdown, and text. Semantic search pulls in the exact data you need — pricing sheets, battle cards, playbooks.

📋

Call Summaries

Auto-generated summaries with action items, key moments, and one-click export to Google Drive or clipboard.

Setup

Plug In Your Keys and Go

Enter your API keys, pick your AI provider, and optionally enable Hume AI for emotion detection. Keys never leave your browser.

New

Read the Room in Real Time

Hume AI analyzes vocal tone to detect how the other person is feeling. A live badge in the overlay header tells you at a glance.

  • Engaged — they're interested, lean into it
  • Frustrated — slow down, acknowledge their concern
  • Thinking — give them space, don't rush
  • Neutral — steady state, carry on
Personas

A Different Expert for Every Call

Each persona bundles a system prompt with its own Knowledge Base documents. Switch context in one click.

  • 12 built-in templates — sales, interview, fundraising, and more
  • Attach PDFs, Markdown, and text files per persona
  • Semantic search pulls relevant data into suggestions
  • Import/export personas with attached documents
Conclave

Multiple Experts, One Call

Activate up to 4 personas at once. Each listens independently and only chimes in when their domain is relevant.

  • Save persona combos as instant-switch presets
  • Set a Conclave Leader to shape post-call summaries
  • Each persona scoped to its own Knowledge Base
  • Quick-activate from the popup dropdown
Post-Call

Never Forget a Follow-Up

When your call ends, Wingman generates a summary with action items and notable moments. One click to copy or save to Drive.

  • Action items tagged by owner (You / Them)
  • Key moments — buying signals, objections, commitments
  • Copy to clipboard or auto-save to Google Drive
  • Google Doc, Markdown, plain text, or JSON format

Your Keys, Your Data, Your Control

Wingman runs entirely in your browser. No backend, no subscription, no data collection. You bring your own API keys — Deepgram (free tier), Gemini (free tier), OpenRouter, Groq, and optionally Hume AI. You control your costs and your privacy.
