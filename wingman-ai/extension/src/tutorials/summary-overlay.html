Using the Summary Overlay

How to view, copy, and interact with your post-call summary

1
Summary Generation Starts

When your call ends, a "Generating Summary..." overlay appears in the top-right corner with an animated loading indicator. Wingman is analyzing your conversation with Gemini.

Note: This only appears if "Auto-generate call summary" is enabled in your settings. The generation typically completes within a few seconds.
2
Summary Card Appears

Once ready, the overlay shows your full "Call Summary" card with a SUMMARY section (key discussion points), ACTION ITEMS (with responsibility labels), a collapsible KEY MOMENTS section, and a Copy button.

Layout: The card header shows "Call Summary" with a green dot (success) and a close (X) button. The body has collapsible sections you can expand or collapse.
3
Review Action Items

The ACTION ITEMS section is visible by default. Each item has a badge showing who is responsible — "YOU" (orange) for your follow-ups and "THEM" (teal) for the other party's commitments.

Tip: Use the Copy button to paste these action items directly into your CRM, email follow-up, or task manager.
4
Expand Key Moments

Click the "KEY MOMENTS" header to expand it. This reveals notable quotes and signals from the conversation, each with a colored border indicating significance.

Color coding: Green borders indicate positive signals (budget, timeline). Red borders flag competitive mentions or objections. Yellow borders highlight scheduling or next steps.
5
Copy to Clipboard

Click the "Copy" button at the bottom of the card. The button changes to "Copied!" confirming the full summary has been copied to your clipboard in a clean, formatted text.

6
Button Resets

After a moment, the button reverts back to "Copy" so you can copy again if needed.

7
Google Drive Auto-Save

If you have Google Drive connected, a "Saved to Drive" indicator appears confirming your transcript (including the summary) was automatically saved.

Note: The Drive file includes the full transcript plus the summary and action items. Click the X button to dismiss the overlay when done.
